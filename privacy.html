Privacy Statement

Last updated: July 13, 2026

ArcoPraxis does not collect any personal information. Ever. Your practice data stays on your device unless you choose to share it.

What we collect

Nothing. ArcoPraxis does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics SDKs, no advertising trackers, and no telemetry sent to our servers — or anyone else’s.

Where your data lives

Scores, practice settings, and any files you import are stored locally on your iPad. Score recognition and playback run entirely on-device. Your music never leaves your device unless you start a share yourself.

Sharing

If you use the system share sheet (or another export action you start), you choose what to share and where it goes. ArcoPraxis does not upload anything in the background.

Device permissions

The app may ask for access to features such as the camera, photo library, files, or microphone so you can import scores and practice on your device. Those permissions are used only for features you use, processed on-device, and never used to collect data about you.

Purchases

Purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Children

Because we collect no personal information, ArcoPraxis does not knowingly gather data from children or anyone else.

Changes

If this statement ever changes, we will update the date above. The core promise remains: no information is collected, and your data stays on your device unless you share it.

Contact

Questions about this privacy statement or the app? Use the form below. Submitting the form sends only what you type — name, email, and message — so we can reply. The ArcoPraxis app itself still collects nothing.
